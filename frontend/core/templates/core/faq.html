Frequently Asked Questions

MpoxTDB is a comprehensive database of multiple types of tandem repeats predicted in the MPXV genomes that taxonomically belong to the two clades (I and II) and four subclades (Ia, Ib, IIa, and IIb).

As of August 2025, MpoxTDB contains a total of SSRs (52,682), cSSRs (1,408), ISSRs (726,636), and VNTRs (86,827) predicted in a total of 6,735 MPXV genomes.

There are multiple reports available on the short tandem repeats in the MPXV genomes. With no database available containing information on tandem repeats (SSRs, cSSRs, iSSRs, and VNTRs), we anticipate that this comprehensive database will serve as a Clade-wise compendium of SSRs, cSSRs, iSSRs, and VNTRs in the MPXV genomes.

If you have a MPXV genome or list of the tandem repeats you can submit it to us through the Contact page. We will be happy to add your data to the MpoxTDB.

MpoxTDB is a user-friendly database, for how to use you can go through the user manual available on the database website.
